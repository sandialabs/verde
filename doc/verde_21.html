Interface Checks

Verde 2.6 also provides tools to verify the interfaces between sections of a model. Figure * shows red squares at locations where two nodes are coincident. This may not necessarily be an error in the model if the nodes are in sliding contact or are constrained by some type of multi-point constraint.

Display of coincident nodes in model.
 

Verde also detects coincident quadrilaterals and triangles. This may indicate that the model is not correctly joined.

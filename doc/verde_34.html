Interface Checks.

Verde 2.5 also provides tools to verify the interfaces between sections of a model. Figure * shows red squares at locations where two nodes are coincident. This may not necessarily be an error in the model if the nodes are in sliding contact or are constrained by some type of multi-point constraint.

Display of coincident nodes in model.
 

If coincident nodes occur in the model, there may also be coincident edges. Coincident edges occur if both endpoints are coincident with another edge. This may indicate that the model is non-conformal.
